Sandwich Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 2 slices of cheese
  • 1 tomato (sliced)
  • 1 cucumber (sliced)
  • 1 tablespoon mayonnaise
  • 1 tablespoon mustard sauce
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Butter for grilling

Instructions

  1. Spread mayonnaise and mustard sauce on one side of both bread slices.
  2. Layer cheese, tomato slices, and cucumber slices on one slice of bread.
  3. Sprinkle salt and pepper to taste.
  4. Place the second slice of bread on top to make a sandwich.
  5. Heat a pan and add a little butter.
  6. Grill the sandwich on both sides until golden brown and crispy.
  7. Serve hot with your favorite sauce or ketchup.
